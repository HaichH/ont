Homepage

As you enter the website for the first time, that page is known as the homepage or landing page, you can choose what you want to see on that page, customize the page to match your current trending outfits and mood. Be more local to your clients and refresh content to keep the site alive
Things you can edit or add:

  • Picture&matching text that slides in an out
  • Quick links with images that are in blocks just below the sliding images
  • Top 16 pictures per category just beneath the blocks with the heading "product overview
  • Which categories you'd like to show for the "product overview"

 Click Pencil icon to access these or on pages dropdown click homepage

Product categories, sizes and colours

  • Add or Edit categories

  • Add or edit sizes
  • Add or edit how much quantity
